PayPal is the safer, easier way to pay online. Speed through checkout with PayPal without having to share your financial information with merchants.

Using PayPal

The first time you pay with PayPal, you will automatically be registered for a PayPal account (free of charge). You will need to enter your credit or debit card details to complete your purchase. Your card details will then be securely saved in your PayPal account. Now you’re all set up to buy things on Skype, eBay and other websites that accept PayPal.

To protect our customers and ourselves from fraud, restrictions may apply when using your PayPal account to purchase Skype Credit. These restrictions vary depending on the circumstance, and can cause your payment to fail. For instance, if you have more than one Skype Name, you can only link your PayPal account with one Skype account. If you try to buy credit for a different Skype Name, the payment won’t go through.

Monthly purchase limits apply to purchases made through PayPal. Sign in to your Account to view you monthly purchase limits.

You can also enable Auto-Recharge with PayPal so you’ll always have enough credit in your account.
